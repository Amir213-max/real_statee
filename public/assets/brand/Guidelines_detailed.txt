Logo Concept
+ +
The circular shape signifies unity, continuity, and the seamless connection between the two nations through the Red Sea.
The Red Sea emblem highlights the geographical significance of Yafel’s operations, reinforcing trust and credibility.
The Yafel typography is crafted to exude elegance and sophistication, ensuring a timeless and premium brand identity.
