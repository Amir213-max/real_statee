Inspired By The Redsea
Logo Concept
+ +
The circular shape signiﬁes unity, continuity, and the seamless connection between the two nations through the Red Sea.
The Red Sea emblem highlights the geographical signiﬁcance of Yafel’s operations, reinforcing trust and credibility.
The Yafel typography is crafted to exude elegance and sophistication, ensuring a timeless and premium brand identity.
